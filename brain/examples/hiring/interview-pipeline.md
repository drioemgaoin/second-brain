# Interview pipeline (typical stages)

A common multi-stage hiring flow:

1. **Recruiter intro** — HR or a recruiter reaches out; quick fit check.
2. **Screen call** — light technical or role questions.
3. **Technical round** — deeper problem, take-home, or live exercise.
4. **Team / panel** — meet the people you would work with.
5. **Offer** — terms, salary, start date.

Track each stage as its own note, tagging the company and people. The same company
and recruiter recur across stages, so you can later ask "where am I with each
company, and who do I owe a reply to?"
